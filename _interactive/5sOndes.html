Le festival se déroulant dans des carrières souterraines, j’ai créé deux installations cachées, à découvrir au dé- tour d’une galerie. Une radio diﬀusant, à la place des stations, des discours retraçant l’histoire du 20e siècle, et un télégraphe déclenchant des signaux lumineux au fond d’une galerie aﬀaissée . The Château de Fontainebleau museum created a temporary exhibition to showcase a special piece of furniture : an ebony cabinet from the 17th century, decorated with engravings depicting the Odyssey and the life of Alexander The Great. The engravings were so fine, it was impossible to see the details without getting too close.

The museum then asked us to create interactive tables and an app, with which the visitors could zoom in on ultra high definition photos of the cabinet. This was used to link the engravings to their preparatory drawings, also shown at the exhibit. {% include gallery %}
