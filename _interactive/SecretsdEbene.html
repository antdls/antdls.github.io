The engravings were so fine, it was impossible to see the details without getting too close.

The museum then asked us to create interactive tables and an app, with which the visitors could zoom in on ultra high definition photos of the cabinet. This was used to link the engravings to their preparatory drawings, also shown at the exhibit.
