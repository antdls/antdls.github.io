--- title: "Percephone Sync" excerpt: "An attempt at making a centralised synchonisation system for DJs/VJs." header: # image: /assets/images/unsplash-gallery-image-1.jpg teaser: assets/images/Percephone/header-th.jpg gallery: - url: /assets/images/Percephone/image1.jpg image_path: assets/images/Percephone/image1.jpg alt: "First half" - url: /assets/images/Percephone/image2.jpg image_path: assets/images/Percephone/image2.jpg alt: "Second half" --- End-of-studies research project at my engineering school, the goal being to synchronise as much audio/video/data streams as possible. What we actually tried to do was to create a software and hardware system to synchonise data coming from Traktor turntables, video played by VJing software and sequences of scene lights animation.

We didn't manage to make everything work together, but we did create :
- a system to control scene lights with just a computer, which we improved and later used on the Gedeon project;
- a link to control a video playback with Tratkor turntables, which gave a DJ the ability to do some scratching on the music and on the video at the same time. {% include gallery %}
