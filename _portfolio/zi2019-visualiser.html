--- title: "TopographX" excerpt: "iEEG data visualiser for cognitive research." header: image: /assets/images/Visualiser/header.jpg teaser: assets/images/Visualiser/header-th.jpg sidebar: - title: "Client" text: "Research team from the INCC at the Paris Descartes university" - title: "Status" text: "Working Beta version" --- TopographX is a software for iEEG data visualization. It can process and display formatted data extracted from electrodes located in patients' brains and create a 3D representation of it.

It can also create files that can be read by a web player, in order to share results and act as figures in research papers and theses. An example of it can be found HERE
