We wanted to focus on exploring and the feeling of being lost and threatened.

It's in a really unfinished state, with no end except death. We didn't test it much so it might be really hard (?). We didn't have time to implement the story either, though we're planning to complete it eventually.

Story intro
You wake up in the middle of the night, disoriented. The light of your candle feels soothing. Once it fades, you sense a danger lurking in the dark. The shadows feel familiar, yet this makes you wary, not calm.

Controls
WASD : movement
E : pickup objects/open doors
Mouse left : attack
