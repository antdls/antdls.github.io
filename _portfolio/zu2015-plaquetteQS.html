Specialized in creating custom devices, software and installations, our aim was to make our expertise in new technologies and engineering available to artists, events and interior designers. We focused mainly on lighting.

We researched and produced a number of projects such as interactive lights synchronised to a band's instruments, control systems for giant LED screens (for architectural lighting), complete solutions for interactive retail spaces, software interfaces to control lights in concert venues and interactive lights for opera and ballets.
